About the Project

Business Problem

Restaurants and aggregators struggle to understand what drives ratings, revenue, and delivery efficiency across cities and cuisines.

Objectives

Analyze ratings, pricing, delivery time & cuisine trends; predict popularity; surface AI-driven business recommendations.

Dataset

Swiggy restaurant dataset with 1000+ restaurants across major Indian cities, including price, ratings, cuisine & delivery time.

Challenges

Messy multi-format cuisine fields, missing values, inconsistent delimiters and outlier prices required robust cleaning.

KPIs

Total restaurants, avg rating, avg cost, avg delivery time, top cuisines, top cities & customer satisfaction.

Expected Outcomes

Actionable insights, predictive popularity scoring & an interactive decision-support dashboard.
